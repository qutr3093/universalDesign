About Me

Hello there! I'm a passionate undergraduate student at the University of Colorado Boulder, majoring in Creative & Technology Design with the graduating class of 2024. Originally hailing from vibrant Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, my journey in the realm of UX/UI design has been a captivating exploration of the intersection between technology and human experience.

Passionate about crafting accessible and visually captivating design solutions that enhance the user experience. With a relentless commitment to user-centered design principles, I strive to create intuitive and engaging interfaces that leave a lasting impression. My journey as a UX/UI designer is marked by a dedication to understanding user needs, empathizing with their perspectives, and translating those insights into seamless and aesthetically pleasing digital experiences.
